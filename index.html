Why we created this ?

"We created this platform with a clear mission – to save valuable time for our respected teachers. We understand the demands on their schedules and aim to alleviate the cumbersome task of test session creation. By handling this on their behalf, we free up their time for more meaningful and impactful work. Additionally, our platform empowers students to directly focus on preparing for their final board exams without the added stress of test session preparation. It's about making education more efficient and effective for both teachers and students."

From where we have made ?

"We made this thing to help students with their exams. We looked at how the questions are usually asked in exams by different boards. We want students to feel like they're used to the way questions are asked in their actual exams.Our questions are chosen carefully. We asked good teachers what questions are really important and often come up in exams. This way, students can practice and not feel stressed about questions they've never seen before. So, we're here to l exams a bit easier and less scary for students. "
